Projects

Athlete Training Prediction

Time Series, Data Cleaning, Pymc3 Implementation

Built a predictive model to forecast athlete performance on race day, from previous training efforts.

I gathered real world training data from multiple athletes and created a pipeline to import and transform data for prediction of 67 different race points, based on 10,068 training entries. Data was input from 3 main sources, TrainingPeaks, Strava, and Garmin. After processing the individual workout and race files, I built a prediction engine to estimate the speed at which an athlete could race according to their training.

Using statsmodels and PyMC3 I was able to predict most athlete's times within :20s/mile, beating my benchmark of the online calculators many athletes use currently. Check out my project on GitHub below:

Training Prediction Github Repo

Washington D.C. STAR Rating Project

Group Project, Flask App, Website

This project focuses on the metrics used to evaluate schools in the DC Public School system. It examines which factors had the biggest influence on DC public school STAR ratings in the 2010s. The project also identified areas of improvement to better serve the educational needs of DC students. Are there influencial factors that exist outside of those currently considered by the DC Board of Education in their current calculation model for STAR ratings? As part of a team, I examined these and other trends, created a flask app and a presentation in the form of a website. Take a look at the project here:
